Project Details

This art gallery website was created for a family member to have an online space to display her paintings. I created this website very early in my journey to becoming a developer. Because it was early in my journey, I decided to use WordPress, Elementor and an e-commerce template from Ocean WP. The use of WordPress as a CMS ensures that the website owner is able to update the art inventory on their own without having to rely on a developer to maintain the content, which is what they wanted. This was an important factor when determining the technologies used for building the website.

Although this website was created using an Ocean WP template, the process of deploying it with a hosting provider and customizing it to what I needed really taught me a lot. This was my first experience working directly with a hosting provider to deploy a live website and my first experience using WordPress. Modifying an e-commerce store theme to repurpose it as an art gallery with the cart and purchasing features turned off was a great challenge for me at this stage in my learning. In addition, learning how the e-commerce products are maintained through WooCommerce and the product dashboard was really interesting for me.

After the website was completed I provided easy to follow, detailed instructions on how to update the WooCommerce products so the artist is able to manage the website inventory without assistance. I am, of course, always available to assist with any questions and to take care of the more complicated tasks.

I am currently providing ongoing maintenance and updates for this website and technical support on an as needed basis.
