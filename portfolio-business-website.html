Project Details

I created this website for my own web development business. When first starting out on my web development journey I thought it was important to create my own business to help myself learn by working on real projects and websites. For me, the process of figuring out real world solutions to real world problems is the best way to learn and grow my skills. I also really enjoy the design and development process when creating something to help someone else showcase their business or organization, and starting my own small business was a great way to do that while also gaining valuable experience.

I really enjoyed creating my own website, although I did find it challenging to decide on the design and branding. It's somehow easier to help others with those decisions than to make them for myself.
